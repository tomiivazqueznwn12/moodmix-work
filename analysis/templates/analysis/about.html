Welcome to MOODMIX.

A collaborative framework, built into a website application for our Technical Project, MOODMIX is a music recommendation system that works on the amalgamation of machine learning, data science and music. The platform uses Natural Language Processing to do a sentimental analysis of user-inputted texts, which is then labelled according to one of three classes - Positive, Negative and Neutral. Once labelled, the platform runs through its data repository, currently a public Spotify song dataset available on Kaggle, to pick out songs from a wide range of years which complement the label. These songs are then given in a list as the output, with details such as the artist(s), year of release and a Spotify widget that plays the song exclusively for you

We consider our platform to be a small glimpse of the unending possibilities of the near future in music and technology - a realm we consider unexplored to the fullest. This, unsurprisingly, was the motivation behind our project - a new, simple way of absorbing musical content, harnessed specifically for your current mood, while discovering a plethora of new music that you otherwise might not have come across.

As we like to believe, musical taste might be subjective, but the taste for music is undying and evergrowing. And this is a cog that fits right into a musical machine for the future.
